Marc Quinn

Sculpter & Painter Artist

Bio

Marc Quinn (British, born 1964) is a leading contemporary artist.
He first came to prominence in the early 1990s, when he and several
peers redefined what it was to make and experience contemporary art.
Marc Quinn makes art about what it is to be a person living in the
world whether it concerns man's relationship with nature and how that
is mediated by human desire; or what identity and beauty mean and why
people are compelled to transform theirs; or representing current,
social history in his work. His work also connects frequently and
meaningfully with art history, from Modern masters right back to
antiquity.
